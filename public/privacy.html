Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

They Won't Shut Up is an AI voice hotline operated by Newth.ai. This policy describes how we collect and use information when you interact with the hotline and this website.

Information We Collect

Phone number: When you call +1 (855) 580-0508, your phone number is captured by our telephony provider (Twilio) to route the call. If you opt in to SMS, your number is used to send follow-up messages.

Call data: Call duration and timestamps are logged for service quality purposes. Conversations with AI characters may be processed in real time but are not stored or recorded by default.

Website: This website does not use cookies, does not require accounts, and does not collect personal information.

How We Use Your Information

Phone numbers are used solely for delivering the voice hotline service and optional SMS follow-up messages. We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

SMS Messaging

If you opt in to SMS (by calling the hotline), you may receive up to 5 messages per month. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. See our SMS Consent page for full details.

Third-Party Services

Twilio provides telephony and SMS services. See Twilio's Privacy Policy.

ElevenLabs provides AI voice technology. See ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy.

Vercel hosts this website. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Phone numbers are retained only as long as needed to provide the service. If you opt out of SMS, your number is removed from our messaging list.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact oliver@newth.ai.

Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will appear on this page with an updated revision date.
