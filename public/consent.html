About This Service

They Won't Shut Up is an AI voice hotline operated by Newth.ai. Users call +1 (855) 580-0508 to have conversations with AI characters powered by ElevenLabs Conversational AI.

What Messages You May Receive

By opting in, you consent to receive the following types of SMS messages from +1 (855) 580-0508:

  • Call follow-up messages (e.g., a summary or check-in after your call)
  • Service notifications (e.g., updates about the hotline or new characters)

Messages are sent at a frequency of no more than 5 messages per month. Message and data rates may apply.

How to Opt In

You opt in to receive SMS messages by calling the hotline at +1 (855) 580-0508. By placing a call, you consent to receive follow-up text messages at the phone number you called from.

By calling +1 (855) 580-0508, you agree to receive automated SMS messages from They Won't Shut Up (Newth.ai). You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. Message frequency varies, up to 5 msgs/month. Msg & data rates may apply.

How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message from +1 (855) 580-0508
  • Emailing oliver@newth.ai with your phone number and a request to unsubscribe

After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
